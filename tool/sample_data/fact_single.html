Told in his own words and by those closest to him, HAWKING follows Professor Stephen Hawking’s journey from boyhood underachiever, to PhD genius, to being diagnosed with Motor Neurone disease and given just two years to live. Despite the constant threat of death, Hawking has risen to fame and international recognition and continues to make scientific discoveries.
